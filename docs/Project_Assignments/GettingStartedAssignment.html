Lists¶

In other words show that as \(h\) gets smaller and smaller \(\frac{\ln(1+h)-\ln(1)}h\) approaches 1. Mathematically we write this as $\( \lim_{h\to 0} \frac{\ln(1+h)-\ln(1)}h = 1 \)$
